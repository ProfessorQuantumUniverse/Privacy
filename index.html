Privacy Policy

For the mobile application "Phantasialand queue times"

Your privacy is not just a policy; it's our core principle. This document outlines our unwavering commitment to protecting your personal information by not collecting it in the first place.

1. The Zero-Data Collection Guarantee

This application has been designed from the ground up with your privacy as the highest priority. We state this in the clearest terms possible:

This app collects, stores, shares, or transmits absolutely NO personal data or sensitive user information.

Your usage of this app is completely anonymous. To be perfectly clear, here is a non-exhaustive list of data we DO NOT collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any other contact information.
  • Your device's unique identifiers (Device ID, Advertising ID, etc.).
  • Your precise or approximate location.
  • Your contacts, calendars, or call logs.
  • Your photos, videos, or any files stored on your device (unless you explicitly interact with them within the app's functionality).
  • Any usage analytics, crash reports that contain personal data, or telemetry of any kind.

2. Application Permissions

If the application requests any permissions (such as camera or storage access), it is solely to enable its core, offline functionality. For example, a "Save" feature requires storage permission to save a file on your device.

Any data accessed via these permissions remains exclusively on your device. It is never sent to us or any third party. It is processed locally and ephemerally for the immediate purpose of the feature you are using.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries for the purposes of analytics, advertising, or user tracking. The app is self-contained to ensure your data stays your own.

4. Open Source Transparency (FOSS)

This app is Free and Open Source Software (FOSS). We believe in full transparency. You can inspect our source code at any time to verify our "no data" claims and ensure there are no hidden trackers or data collection mechanisms.

5. Changes to This Policy

Given our strict no-data policy, we do not foresee any substantive changes to this privacy policy. Should any updates be necessary, they will be published here. We will never change our core principle of not collecting user data.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our practices, please do not hesitate to contact us.
